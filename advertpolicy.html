Advertising Policy

This article describes our advertising policies.

  1. AdCafé reserves the right to decline any type of advertising that is damaging to the brand of AdCafé or is inappropriate to the content held on the AdCafé app.
  2. AdCafé does not accept advertising of any product or service that is declared illegal by the Government of the Republic Of Kenya.
  3. Advertisements may not be deceptive or misleading, and must be verifiable. Advertisements should clearly identify the advertiser and the product or service being offered. Exaggerated or extravagantly worded copy will not be allowed. Advertisements will not be accepted if they appear to be indecent or offensive in either text or artwork, or if they relate to content of a personal, racial, ethnic, political, sexual orientation, or religious nature.
  4. Once the advertisement is uploaded to the app, it can only be taken down by the uploader or publisher before the date of showcasing.
  5. AdCafé can take down an advertisement if it does not adhere to the policies of advertising in the app.
  6. Uploading of an advertisement similar to previously showcased advert in the app is not accepted.
  7. Advertisements that asserts, implies or showcases personal attributes is not accepted.
  8. AdCafé prohibits the promotion of products or services in the following categories:
    • Adult or sexual products and services
    • Endangered species products
    • Hateful content
    • Profanity
    • Third-party infringement
    • Misleading or false content
    • Controversial content
    • Toxic foodstuffs or supplements
    • Discrimminatory practices
    • Inappropriate content
    • Illegal products and services
    • Body branding such as tattooing or permanent cosmetics
    • Political campaining or any political practices
    • Weapons, weapon accessories and weapon usage based services
    • Spyware and illegal hacking software
    • Adult or sexual products and services
    • Phishing and malware software
    • Surveillence or spying equipment
    • Non-existent information such as blank advertisements
    • Counterfeit documents and other items
    • Multilevel marketing
  9. AdCafé is not liable for any effect, occurrence or outcome arising as a result of advertising in the app.
